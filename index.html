About

I work as an SRE / Cloud Engineer focused on keeping systems reliable as they grow. My day-to-day centers around Kubernetes, AWS, Infrastructure as Code, and observability — keeping deployments predictable and giving teams a platform they can ship on without friction. I care about performance, clean operations, and systems that hold up under pressure.

I graduated from the Computer Infrastructure Analyst program at ORT University (Uruguay). That foundation shaped how I approach problems: think critically, decide with incomplete information, and keep things practical. I’m most comfortable debugging complex issues, simplifying architecture, and turning uncertainty into something that works.
